A. DECLARATION OF NEW / RE-ASSESSMENT OF BUILDINGS AND MACHINERIES / VERIFICATION OF BUILDINGS
REQUIREMENTS / PROCESS INPUTS:

1. Accomplished application form

2. Proof of legitimacy of the transaction

2.a For Real Property Owner:

Photocopy of Valid ID

2.b For Authorized Representative:

Authorization Letter duly signed by the Real Property Owner with photocopy of the owner and representative's Valid ID

FOR BUILDING

1. Photocopy of Real Property Tax Receipt for the Current Year (for Land) / Tax Clearance

2. Original copy of Building Plans (if any)

3. Photocopy of Transfer Certificate of Title (TCT) (if there is a need to verify further the property's location vs. the Real Property Tax Receipt presented)

4. Photocopy of Vicinity Map/Building/Occupancy Permit (if any or necessary i.e, the building owner's name is different from the lot owner's name)

FOR MACHINERIES

1. Notarized Sworn Statement of Ownership

2. Official Receipt of the machineries upon purchase (if needed)

3. Itemized List of Machineries (if applicable)

B. TRANSFER OF OWNERSHIP
REQUIREMENTS / PROCESS INPUTS:

1. Accomplished application form

2. New Transfer Certificate of Title (TCT) for presentation and its photocopy or Certified True Copy

3. Photocopy of Deed of Sale (2 copies) / Deed of Donation / Extrajudicial Settlement of Estate / Affidavit of Consolidation on of Ownership with Certificate of Sale or any document to prove ownership thereof

4. Photocopy of Tax Clearance / Real Property tax receipt for the current year

5. Certificate of Authorizing Registration for presentation (if the building to be transferred is not indicated in the Deed of Sale)

Note: In case when the title subject for declaration was issued five (5) years and beyond from the date of declaration, the documents required are 1, 2, 4 only

C. DECLARATION OF CONSOLIDATION / SUBDIVISION OF LANDS
REQUIREMENTS / PROCESS INPUTS:

1. Accomplished application form

2. New Transfer Certificate of Title (TCT) for presentation and its photocopy or Certified True Copy

3. Photocopy of Deed of Sale (2 copies) / Deed of Donation / Extrajudicial Settlement of Estate / Affidavit of Consolidation on of Ownership with Certificate of Sale or any legal document to prove ownership thereof

4. Photocopy of Tax Clearance / Real Property tax receipt for the current year

5. Approved Consolidation / Subdivision Plan (2 copies)

6. Certificate of Authorizing Registration for presentation (if the building to be transferred is not indicated in the Deed of Sale)

ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENT FOR AGRICULTURAL CLASSIFICATION

1. Certified True Copy of SP Resolution for Reclassification / Development Permit / Zoning Certificate

Note: In case when the title subject for declaration was issued five (5) years and beyond from the date of declaration, the documents required are 1, 2, 4, 5, and the additional requirement for agricultural classification.
